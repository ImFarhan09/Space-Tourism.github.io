Space capsule

A space capsule is an often-crewed spacecraft
that uses a blunt-body reentry capsule to
reenter the Earth's atmosphere without wings.
Our capsule is where you'll spend your time
during the flight. It includes a space gym,
cinema, and plenty of other activities to keep
you entertained.
